About Guardian

Get to know the owner

Steve Casey is a licensed termite operator, a licensed general contractor and a certiﬁed home inspector. His background in the termite inspection is not only his profession but also something that has been a tradition business in his family since 1968. His decades of experience and qualifications have made Steve a renowned and trusted inspector in the bay area. Since he has a culmination of 3 generations of experience, Steve provides a unique level of service that integrate the best of traditional termite inspection practices with inspection solutions.
